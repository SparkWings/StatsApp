Welcome to The MCL Stats Tracker


Intro

Hello! If you're here, you're looking to download my MCL tool -- called Stat Tracker. In late 2016 I was approached by the Management team to create this application, which I accepted. Featuring a simplistic design and format, you'll get all the data you need from any MS scrims you provide the program.


What It Does

Basically:

You feed it (the program) a Minecraft Log file which can be found in %appdata%\logs\ OR ~/Library/Application Support/Minecraft/Logs
It then reads the log file and team data you provide it from scrims, which it then pastes in a view-able format for your data crunching.


How To Use

Step 1: Feed it the scrim log file.
Step 2: Enter team data from both teams, including the team names, team members separated by a comma, and in-game team colors. Please keep in mind that when you enter team colors, only enter in the team colors for the first half of play; the computer will handle the rest.
Step 3: Give the program a second to parse the data, and voila ~ Your data is displayed to you on the screen.
