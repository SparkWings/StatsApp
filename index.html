Welcome to The MCL Stats Tracker


Intro

I was approached by the Management team within the MCL and asked to make this application specifically for the MS teams that need to track their team stats. It took me roughly a few hours to design, code, and publish this. I had decided to not release the code publicly until now.


What It Does

Basically, in essence what the application does:

You feed it a Minecraft Log file which can be found in %appdata%\logs\
It then reads the log file and team data you provide it from scrims, which it then pastes in a view-able format for your data crunching.


How To Use

Step 1: Feed it the scrim log file.
Step 2: Enter team data from both teams, including the team names, team members separated by a comma, and in-game team colors.
Step 3: Give the program a second to parse the data, and voila ~ Your data is displayed to you on the screen.
